Hallo griezel! Hier zie je de agenda voor de komende tijd. De locatie wordt doorgestuurd in de groepsapp van Het Spook. Als je nog geen lid bent en je wilt een keer komen kijken, stuur dan op instagram een DM naar ons en we nodigen je vrijblijvend uit!

Hello ghoul! Here you can see the agenda for the coming time. The location will be sent in the whatsapp group chat of Het Spook. If you are not a member yet and want to come take a look, send us a DM on instagram and we will invite you without obligation!



Zine making workshop
08-09-2025
20:30 (St Annastraat 48) Zines (small diy magazines) historically were an important part of radical political movements, as it is an easy and cheap way of spreading information. We will do a workshop on how to make them combined with drinks and snacks!

Movie Night: Pride (2014)
10-09-2025
19:30 (Location TBA) Pride (2014) is a Heartwarming movie about the solidarity between queer activists and striking miners in the 1980s under the rule of Margret Thatcher. Join us to watch this beautiful movie together followed by a lecture about the connection between Queer liberation and class struggle!

Open Drinks Night
15-09-2025
20:30 (Thiemepark) You cannot do the revolution without having a fun drink once in a while. Join us for our open drinks night to get to know our organisation and have fun with fellow comrades! Everyone is welcome!

Socialist City Tour
20-09-2025
13:30 (Central Station) Did you know that Nijmegen has a rich socialist history? Let’s walk together through Nijmegen and talk about the often neglected stories of radical movements, riots and important revolutionary figures that are connected to this beautiful city!

Socialist Pubquiz
24-09-2025
20:00 (Café t’Haantje) A PubQuiz dedicated to all things socialism in a cozy bar in the center of Nijmegen. Join us, to flex with your knowledge or for a unique night out with a revolutionary flair!
